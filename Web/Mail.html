Contact us


For mail concerning GNU TeXmacs, please write to

  • <bugs@texmacs.org> for bug reports.
  • <texmacs-info@texmacs.org> for posting a message of general interest for TeXmacs users.
  • Joris van der Hoeven <vdhoeven@texmacs.org>: the main author.
  • Ralf Treinen <treinen@texmacs.org>: maintainer of the debian package.
  • Fabien Salvi <webmaster@texmacs.org>: the webmaster.

On our website we maintain a page with reported bugs and suggestions by users. We feel free to include your comments (as well as your name) in these web pages or in future web pages of a similar kind. If you have problems with this, you should let us know that by email. On request, we will in particular remove your name and other personal data from our files, except when these data were not obtained from you, but in another legal way (for instance, from your personal webpage).

It is also possible to suscribe to the texmacs-users@texmacs.org mailing list for getting help from (or giving help to) other TeXmacs users. You may also subscribe to the texmacs-info@texmacs.org mailing list for getting information about main novelties about TeXmacs.
